In Pursuit of Identity



With the ever expanding trend of internet culture that shape the generation my age, the drive for originality seems to fade from the minds of our people. As our youths seem to have an affinity to copy what each other deem relevant to the current times, the rise of FOMO as a term had manifested into a culture in itself. I personally am not a contrarian, but I do believe that people my age should try to paint their canvas with their own design. I am not implying that it is wrong to try to fit in with the society or follow trends, but the excessive need for commonality our youths seem to possess started to negatively impact the way young people see themselves especially online. I am preaching my fellow peers to be yourself, be original and as always, be happy with what you are.
