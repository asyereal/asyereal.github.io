Ash's Repo
Poems 76 to 80:
Tunes

Melanchonic rythms
Swaying, in the waves
Psychedelic tunes
Floating, in the mist

Harmonical notes
Memories to save
Melodical tones
A staying gist

Rain

A pour of blessing
Tears of the sky
When droplets are dancing
They fall, from up high
Constant noises
Tappings, an orchestra
A sign of life
Bond

Days went by, realised
The blame, shall be put
Onto mineself,
My arrogance

Dreamt of us, mesmerised
Was I cold, though it is but
An act, out of disturbance
My ignorance

Lovers, we used to be
Feelings, we used to keep
Lovesick, all night's lullaby
Our time, not one shall buy

Precious, dazzling as ruby
Distance, it cuts in deep
Tired, as time goes by
Our fear, to say goodbye
I need you

Hannah
Hannah

A Hannah is a kind
often cheery and
calm natured girl

A Hannah is someone
someone you might find
unique in your mind

Rude!

Rude! You're so rude!
Why can't you be polite
It's your attitude
You think you did it right
The manners, where are they?
I think you threw them away
