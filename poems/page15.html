Ash's Repo
Poems 71 to 75:
Headache

Dread! The feels on my head
Pulsing pain, makes me faint
Headache, for god's sake!
My notion forms a quake
Sticks and stones, break my bones
Route; The pills got me out
Desicion

A question that haunt
The confusion, it taunts
Was not met with clue
'Bout risks, how it grew
For me to decide
Reveal what's inside
In God I confide

I ran back and forth
From South up to North
Just none who can tell
In riddles I fell
Struggling to think well

To choose, or to lose
To try or rely
To make, for your sake:
The Desicion

Unfinished

To forgive is to rely
Nobleness, none can deny
Giving mercy, blessing for one
For what we did, for what they done
Nighttime

Dark blue sky
Tranquility
Underneath the stars
Chandelier
Cricket noises
Symphony
Bright moon
Reflect
Tail

Stop giving me hail
I don't want a tail
Pathetic, how you fail

You're like a tick
Not one shall pick
You make me sick
