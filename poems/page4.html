Poems 16 to 20:
Farewell

Before I say farewell, I have some things to tell
Do not forget our past, for memories will last
To reminisce the best, but don't ignore the rest
I'm grateful to the core, for moments I adore
Dinner

It's something you,
Eat at night
It's something you,
Take a bite
It's in the menu,
Good night
Cooking

Salt, pepper, seasoning
Stove, ladle, pot
Stir, boil, sizzling
Delicious, rather, hot
Tastes, of reality
Colour Pencils

Wooden wonders
Pigment, oil
Various hues
Many contrasts
Oh, the colours of life
Time

Constant marching of history
Secrets, many stories
Never stops, nor reverses
Many dreams, to traverse
Shall waste not, for it's gold
Use it wisely, 'fore you're old
