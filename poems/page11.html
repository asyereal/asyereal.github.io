Poems 51 to 55:
Touch

Your touch is like silk
Gentle, delicate velvet
Touch, a love language
Paraffin

Gasoline, paraffin, kerosene
You're the flame, with a light to be seen
Sprit's burning, a candle of keen
Show them all, what you worth, how you mean

When you shine, you're so fine, just like wine
You're so kind, noble mind, hard to find
An art fiction, a novel to bind
Flame indeed, as a star on the line

My Heart

She broke my heart
It's now mangled hard
She broke my heart
Sweet lies, oh so smart
She broke my heart
Her love was not sincere
She broke my heart
Slowly did it tear
Death

Death
Macabre, morbid
Lying still, unable
Every soul's biggest fear
Mortal
Number

One, the moon and the sun
Two, the monsoon comes in two
Three, clover leaves on it's tree
Four, directions to explore
Five, hibiscus petals when they arrive
