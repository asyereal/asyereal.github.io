Nostalgia

Nostalgia,
Lingering memories
Untold stories

Mesmerising, staring into space
Deep dreaming, mind is in haze
Indeed, nostalgia

Capture

Memory, mind library
Past, thing that passed
Capture, moments of rapture
Lapse, thoughts in perhaps
Ovation, manifests appreciation
Remember, melanchony ember
Recall, down the memory hall
Relive, pain to relive
Electricity

Shock, when I catch yer gaze
Static, like I'm being tazed
Electric, my heart's on a race
Current, running pulse I'm fazed
Love, what we shall embrace
Shine

Emerald, you capture my eyes
Ruby, you strike my heart
Gold, you're rare and wise
Diamond, your shine, it's art
Platinum, your dazzling beauty
Happiness

Delve into emotions
Seeking for happiness
What we all deserve
