Heart's Content

Hidden within
Secrets therein
What makes the heave
Shall take the leave
Verily valued
Clues you pursued
What you conceal
Shall you reveal
None I intend
On your heart's content
Steal

My pencil, for real!?
Oh please, just stop!
The truth, they slop
Your lies, they drop
You better give it back
Or I'll give you a smack
The Test

Lest you feel onto fear
For the test you seest near
'Tis a testament for yer will
Parts of life, living frill
It will pass, be so sure
Few of souls, would endure
'Tis 'bout time, should you see
How divine, one could be
