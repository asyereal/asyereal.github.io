Ash's Repo
Poems 81 to 85:
Heart's Content

Hidden within
Secrets therein
What makes the heave
Shall take the leave
Verily valued
Clues you pursued
What you conceal
Shall you reveal
None I intend
On your heart's content
Steal

Hey what's the deal!?
How dare you steal!?
My pencil, for real!?
Oh please, just stop!
The truth, they slop
Your lies, they drop
You better give it back
Or I'll give you a smack
The Test

Lest you feel onto fear
For the test you seest near
'Tis a testament for yer will
Parts of life, living frill
It will pass, be so sure
Few of souls, would endure
'Tis 'bout time, should you see
How divine, one could be
Grudge

The grudge you betake
False hatred you make
Writing up loathe
Ill willing oath
Darkening ink
Therein thy hearts sink
Let death splits us then
Beginning

To start
A piece of art
So new
As morning dew
Devotion
A sign of emotion
Creation
