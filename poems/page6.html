Poems 26 to 30:
Nightlife

Nightlife, through the city
Nightlife, not a clarity
Nightlife, immortality
Nightlife, don't bring me reality
Bestfriend

Bestfriend
Loyal, always
Appreciate, remember, return
My only one, companion
Remedy
Energy

Burst of energy
Fuel, trigger, move my body
My eyes wide open

Stream of staggery
Motion, means no lethargy
The Beast, awoken

Alone

Barren, remote, vast
Will today, be the last
Surrounded, but still alone
I, am on my own

Screaming, to be unheard of
Present, I'm like the wind
Unnoticed, pushed off
Unclear hint

Sleep

Dreaming so deep
Deep in the sleep

Soaring so high,
High, into the night

Dive in the ocean
Ocean, of emotions

Waking means clarity
Clarity, of bitter reality
