Ash's Repo
Poems 46 to 50:
Music

Music
Harmonious, beautiful
Fiddling, blowing, beating
Virtuoso, serenade, ensemble, score
Rythm
Childhood

My childhood, as I remember
Melancholic, poignant, sombre
Grieving not, I am to be glad,
For childhood is not always sad
What is left, the memories I have
They shall bring me, a crying laugh
My Heart

She broke my heart
It's now mangled hard
She broke my heart
Sweet lies, oh so smart
She broke my heart
Her love was not sincere
She broke my heart
Slowly did it tear
Death

Death
Macabre, morbid
Lying still, unable
Every soul's biggest fear
Mortal
Saint

You've been so hideously charming
so stunned, I couldn't see myself
Your presence seemed so alarming
those eyes forgot where they left

You're such a saint, oh I could faint
you look so just, so I gave you trust
And when I glanced, as you soar
it made me speechless to the core

No fear you bear, unbound to risks
went here and there, no one to miss
Each time you go, you make me cry
deep down you know, no use to lie

When the light starts to shade
love that binds turns to hate
Your time is up, now shall you pay
I can't be stopped, you can only pray
