Real Darknet
Poems 46 to 50:
Music

Music
Harmonious, beautiful
Fiddling, blowing, beating
Virtuoso, serenade, ensemble, score
Rythm
Childhood

My childhood, as I remember
Melancholic, poignant, sombre
Grieving not, I am to be glad,
For childhood is not always sad
What is left, the memories I have
They shall bring me, a crying laugh
My Heart

She broke my heart
It's now mangled hard
She broke my heart
Sweet lies, oh so smart
She broke my heart
Her love was not sincere
She broke my heart
Slowly did it tear
Death

Death
Macabre, morbid
Lying still, unable
Every soul's biggest fear
Mortal
Number

One, the moon and the sun
Two, the monsoon comes in two
Three, clover leaves on it's tree
Four, directions to explore
Five, hibiscus petals when they arrive
