Ash's Repo
Poems 66 to 70:
Stars

Flaming air balls, light
When my heart falls, sight
Mirror we call moon
Mesmerised to soon
Stumbling onto stone
Love when I'm alone
How I gaze, into space

Stratums of the sky
Wander up so high
Clouds of scarlet smoke
Roused, my heart it stoke
Possessing darkness
A hanging lightness

Dancing

Make your body move
Sink into the groove
Rise along the beat
Jiggle, feel the heat
It's time to get low
Dance into the flow
Book

I like reading books
They help me kill time
Always got me hooked
Opens up my mind
I like reading books
Do you?

I love reading books
I get to know things
Books change how I look
Books change how I think
I like to learn more
History and lore

Companions

O companions, shall you be kind
For companions, are hard to find
They will be there, shall you are weak
The joy they share, the one you seek

We all need companions,
No matter the opinions
For we are all humans
Let go, of the arrogance
Perhaps they are few
But sincere and true
Some call them crew
The people around you

Archery

The arrows, a chorus, rain
Daringly bold, gain
Persistent, presicion
