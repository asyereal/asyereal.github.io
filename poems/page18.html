Profanity

See I'm quite
Indecent, unmodest
Judge me for
Obscene, but honest
Call me constipated
I can't give a sh!t
Meet Again

Once again,
we come to meet
Tales to tell
message to read
Until next time
when fate sets us part
Hope to remind
the ones in your heart
Air

The air we all breathe
betaken for granted
Sought seldom, the need
when lost you seek after

The air we inhale
the matter of most
A blessing we fail
to value the flows

Calibre

Trait, quality

My interest, investing in

The end is near
