Darling

My darling, I miss you
My darling, I'm all for you
My darling, I need you
My darling, I'll wait for you
My darling, I love you
My darling, I hope for you
Promise

Promise me, swear to be
Lover eternally
I'll give my all to you
My heart, true and true
There for you

When you're in dread, mad, sad
I'll be there for you
If you're indeed, in need
I'll be next to you
Shall you're below, deep sorrow
I'll go down with you
