Poems 1 to 5:
Her

Her smile warms my heart
Delicate, charming, beauty
Never grow older
Her

Her, it is her
She is my dearest
My thoughts in day
My dreams at night
Unaware she is,
Of my presence
Her

Reminisce, memories
I value, I cherish
All the old stories
How I really wish
To be with her
Gaze

Mingling thoughts
Rushing blood
Blushing face

Tongue in knots
Feeling odd
It's her gaze

I Think

Look once, look again
There is something
tranquil
pleasant
capturing
I think I like her
